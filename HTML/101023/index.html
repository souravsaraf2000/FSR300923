New Cars in India 2023

  1. The Invicto is the largest, the most premium and the most expensive vehicle Maruti Suzuki has ever sold. It's based on the Toyota Innova Hycross and follows the same principles, offering lots of space, features and a comfortable ride. The Invicto's interior lacks the premium look and feel its high price demands although you still get the same Toyota-developed hybrid system which makes it incredibly fuel efficient.

  2. The Kona Electric is a good effort by Hyundai to offer an EV at an accessible price point. Coupled with an ARAI certified range of 452km on a single charge, the Kona Electric has you covered for your daily intra-city travels. However, the lack of sufficient charging infrastructure means that this EV cannot be your only car in the household. Rumours have it that the new Kona is due in India in the coming months.
